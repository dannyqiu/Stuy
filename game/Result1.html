Stuy
  • Game - Adventure Time
You make your way slowly
down the tunnel.
As you walk, you notice the air
around you get slowly hotter and hotter.
Then you look down to see a bright
light shining from beneath it.
Too late, you realize that the floor
you are standing on is but a weak
covering of a cavern of magma.
Moving quickly, you try to get out of
there, but the floor collapses, and
a quick plummet is the end.
